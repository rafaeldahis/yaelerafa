YAEL & RAFA

NOVEMBER 19th, 2016 - Rio de Janeiro

We're excited to celebrate our wedding with you!

Here's a summary of our 7-year story :)

We’ve known each other for many, many years - but never imagined we would date. We went to the same high school, we had friends in common, and Rafa’s mom, Clarice, was the Principal of our school.

However, it was’t until 2009, when Yael went to a school trip to Israel with Rafa’s mom that we got to know each other better. Clarice started talking to Rafa about Yael and opened his eyes for what he was missing. A few months later, Rafa gave Yael a ride coming back from a party and boom - the first kiss happened!

We started dating and, since then, we have traveled, hung out with friends and family, enjoyed life together in Rio. In September 2014, we were getting ready to move to San Francisco, California, and Rafa finally popped the question! Of course Yael said yes, because here we are.

We moved in September to work and study and explore California. We traveled, we made good new friends, we bought and assembled our furniture. We also had the amazing chance to host and show SF around to great friends and family from Brazil who came to visit. In January 2015 we adopted a cat, Katie, who always says good morning and cuddles between us while watching Netflix. We even got married (already?)!

And now it’s time to celebrate with all of you this love story and many more years and adventures to come!
Please join us in Rio for an unforgettable night!

VISAS

American citizens need visas to get into Brazil. For more information please visit this website. If you hold another passport, please check out a list of countries which are exempted from visas (including citizens of the EU, Israel, Switzerland and Latin American countries).
